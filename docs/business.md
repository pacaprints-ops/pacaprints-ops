# Paca — Business Overview

## The Business
- **Name:** Pacaprints
- **Formerly:** The Digital Vibe (rebranding Jan 2025 → Pacaprints, completing ~Dec 2026)
- **Website:** pacaprints.com
- **Based:** Bournemouth, UK
- **Started:** January 2025

## The Team
- **Carrie** — Ops, business, website, tech
- **Vicky** — Design, socials, order fulfilment

## What We Do
We design and print 99% of our products ourselves. We also personalise items using a Cricut machine. Small extras (frames, gift bundle items) are purchased in.

## Products

### Prints
- Quote prints
- Photo prints
- Personalised prints
- Keepsake prints
- Wedding signage
- Seasonal prints

### Cards
- Greeting cards

### Seasonal Gifts
- Seasonal bundles (e.g. Mother's Day print + frame, Teacher end-of-school print + plaque + card)

### Cricut / Personalised
- Personalised bags, cups etc. (hen dos, events)
- Kids chore charts
- Brushing teeth charts

### Business Products
- Loyalty cards
- Price lists

## Sales Channels
- **TikTok Shop** — main sales channel
- **Etsy** — partial range
- **eBay**
- **pacaprints.com** — own site

## Customers
Everyday people buying prints, cards and gifts for themselves or as presents.
