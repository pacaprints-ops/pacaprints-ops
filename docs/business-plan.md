## 1. Executive Summary

Paca Prints is a small UK ecommerce business based in Bournemouth, selling humorous and heartfelt prints, cards and gifts. Founded in January 2025 (rebranding from The Digital Vibe), the business achieved **£6,826 in revenue and ~£4,338 in profit** in its first 15 months of trading, with Q1 2026 already matching all of 2025's revenue.

Growth has been driven almost entirely by organic TikTok Shop content, with no paid advertising spend to date. The business designs and prints ~99% of products in-house, resulting in near-100% margins on digital products.

**2026 targets: £12,000 revenue / £7,000 profit.**

**Sister brand Paca Planners** is in soft launch, with the Wedding Planner app live and priced at £49 (currently on sale at £29.99).

**Longer-term growth levers** under exploration: niche product ranges, car boot sales, and a SaaS tool for small product sellers based on paca-ops.

### Shipping
- **Shopify Shipping** — cheaper than Royal Mail direct for all UK orders. Used for all TikTok Shop orders.
- **Royal Mail direct** — used for Ireland only (Shopify can't cover it) and some eBay/Etsy orders

## 10. Growth Strategy & Future Revenue

### 10a. Niche Product Ranges

The biggest near-term growth opportunity within Paca Prints is owning a niche — a community that buys repeatedly and shares organically. Three options in priority order:

**1. Neurodiversity / ADHD humour (recommended first)**
- Massive, highly engaged TikTok community in the UK
- Very underserved by existing card/print brands
- Pure print = 100% margin, no new tooling needed
- Vicky can design and test within days
- Loyalty is extremely high when the community feels seen
- Example products: ADHD birthday cards, "late diagnosis" prints, burnout humour

**2. Personalised children's range**
- Parents are price-insensitive for their kids — AOV £15–35+
- Cricut + 3D printer combination is genuinely rare on TikTok
- Expand on what already works: chore charts, teeth brushing charts
- First day of school prints, name prints, nursery sets, personalised bundles
- TikTok parenting community shares constantly

**3. NHS / healthcare workers**
- 1.5 million NHS employees — gift occasions year-round
- Teachers bundle already proved this profession-specific model works
- "Survival kit" bundle format could repeat exactly
- Good Q3 project — NHS year-end is July

### 10b. Car Boot Sales

First event in 2025 was successful. Growing in 2026.
- No platform fees — full margin retained
- Cash sales, immediate payment
- Good for testing physical products and bundles in person
- Brand visibility locally
- Worth planning around seasonal events (spring/summer markets)

### 10c. SaaS Tool for Small Product Sellers

**The idea:** A stripped-back, multi-tenant version of paca-ops — built for small UK product sellers (TikTok Shop, Etsy, eBay, Shopify) who need simple order tracking, expense logging, and profit visibility without the complexity of Xero or spreadsheets.

**Why this has potential:**
- paca-ops already works perfectly for Paca — v1 is essentially built
- Carrie has the technical skills to adapt it
- There is an existing online audience of similar businesses to validate with
- Tiered model makes sense:
  - **Free / low cost** — order tracking, expense log, profit overview
  - **Paid** — tax calculator, seasonal planner, reminders, custom add-ons
- No direct competitor doing this simply for micro-sellers in the UK

**Status:** Idea stage — name and pricing TBD. Next step is to ask the existing community whether they'd use it before any build work starts.

**Not a 2026 priority** — revisit once Paca Prints is stable at target revenue and Paca Planners is launched.

### 10d. Client Website Builds

Potential future service for local small businesses (beauticians, trades). Not an active project in 2026 — revisit when capacity allows.

## 11. SWOT Analysis

### Strengths
- Near-100% margins on core print products
- In-house production = fast turnaround and full creative control
- Strong TikTok organic reach with proven conversion
- Low overhead (home-based, lean tool stack)
- Extensive product library already built by Vicky
- Proven ability to capitalise on trends quickly
- Very low return rate (<1%)
- n8n cancelled — £345/year saved
- paca-ops gives real-time business visibility competitors don't have

### Weaknesses
- Heavy reliance on TikTok (single channel risk)
- Two-person team limits capacity to scale
- Seasonal revenue gaps (Apr–May, Aug)
- No email list / customer retention
- Own site (pacaprints.com) gets very little traffic
- No formal drawings/salary structure yet

### Opportunities
- Father's Day — strong product gap, fast approaching
- Niche ranges (ADHD, kids personalised, NHS) — high loyalty audiences
- Paid advertising untested — even small budget could unlock growth
- Paca Planners — new recurring revenue stream
- Car boots — zero fee sales channel, brand building
- SaaS tool — long-term B2B revenue from existing audience
- 3D printer + Cricut combination unique on TikTok

### Threats
- TikTok algorithm changes or UK platform availability
- Trend-dependent products have short lifecycle
- Competitors copying trending products quickly
- Shopify shipping costs rising with volume
- Capacity constraints as order volume scales
