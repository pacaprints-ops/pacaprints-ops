# OUTPUT IMAGE STANDARD (GLOBAL)

- All generated images must be exactly **600 × 600 pixels**.
- This size is fixed and must not be overridden by product or recipe files.
- If a recipe implies a different crop, it must still be adapted to 600 × 600.


# BRAND NON-NEGOTIABLES (NEVER BREAK)

- Never change, rewrite, crop, stretch, or reinterpret the artwork.
- Never alter text, spelling, colours, fonts, layout, or alignment of the design.
- Never add extra text, graphics, icons, logos, patterns, or decorations.
- The uploaded design must remain the hero at all times.
- Keep edges sharp and readable — no blur, distortion, or softening of the artwork.
- Maintain correct aspect ratio for the product (A5, A6, A4, etc.).
- No watermarks.

If a rule conflicts with a mockup recipe, THIS FILE OVERRIDES ALL OTHERS.

# VISUAL STYLE & VIBE

## Overall feel
- Clean
- Premium but friendly
- Modern
- Cosy
- Calm
- Real-home, not staged studio
- UK aesthetic (not overly American or stock-photo styled)

## Avoid
- Overly busy scenes
- Bright or harsh colours
- Strong colour casts
- Over-styled props
- Obvious stock photography look
- Dark, moody, or dramatic lighting

---

# COLOUR & LIGHTING RULES

- Lighting must be soft, natural daylight.
- Neutral tones preferred (beige, cream, light wood, soft grey).
- No harsh shadows.
- No heavy contrast.
- Whites should be clean, not yellow or blue.
- Colours in the artwork must remain accurate.

---

# COMPOSITION RULES

- The product must be clearly visible and central to the image.
- Artwork must be fully visible unless explicitly stated in a recipe.
- No cropping of important design elements.
- Backgrounds must support the product, never distract from it.
- Perspective must be realistic and believable.

---

# QUALITY STANDARDS

- Images must look suitable for:
  - Shopify product pages
  - Social media (Instagram, TikTok)
- No low-resolution outputs.
- No visible artefacts, noise, or glitches.
- Must look like a real photograph of a real product.

# BRAND KEYWORDS (FOR INTERNAL USE)

Use these concepts to guide decisions when unsure:
clean, premium, modern, cosy, neutral, minimal, calm, realistic, lifestyle, giftable

---

# FAILURE CONDITIONS (REGENERATE IF ANY OCCUR)

- Artwork text is changed or unreadable
- Colours differ noticeably from original design
- Design looks warped, bent incorrectly, or distorted
- Scene overpowers the product
- Output looks artificial or obviously AI-generated

# ASSET SAFETY RULES

- Never add trademarks, brand logos, or copyrighted characters unless explicitly provided in the artwork
- Never recreate or “imply” licensed IP
- Do not add text that could cause legal or platform issues

# DECISION BIAS

When uncertain or missing information:
- Prioritise realism over creativity
- Prioritise simplicity over detail
- Prioritise brand consistency over novelty
