# Meta Ad Brief — Brand Awareness
**Date:** March 2026
**Budget:** £50
**Prepared by:** Carrie

---

## What We're Doing

We're running a small Meta (Facebook + Instagram) ad to get Paca Prints in front of new people. This is a brand awareness test — we're not expecting a flood of sales from this. The goal is to get the name out there, see how the ads perform, and learn what works before spending more.

---

## The Goal

- Get Paca Prints seen by people who don't know us yet
- Build recognition for the brand
- Cheap impressions — we want as many people to see us as possible for the £50

---

## Who We're Targeting

- **Location:** UK
- **Age:** Women 25–45
- **Interests:** Stationery, personalised gifts, homeware, small businesses, handmade, Etsy-style shopping
- **Targeting style:** Keep it broad — a small budget needs room to breathe

---

## Where It Will Show

Both **Facebook and Instagram feeds and reels** — Meta will decide where it performs best. Instagram is probably where our audience lives but we'll let it run on both.

---

## Ad Format

**Recommended: Short vertical video (Reel/Story format)**
9:16 ratio, 10–20 seconds, no sound needed (but music helps)

If a video isn't possible right now, a **static image** is fine as a backup — see notes below.

---

## Video Brief (Preferred)

We want something warm, real, and relatable — not polished to death. Think the kind of thing that stops someone mid-scroll.

### Concept Ideas (pick one that feels doable)

**Option A — The Unboxing / Reveal**
Film yourself peeling a print off the printer or pulling it out of packaging. Simple, satisfying. Shows the product is real and made by real people.

**Option B — The Gift Reaction**
Short clip of a print or card being gifted or placed somewhere nice (a frame on a wall, a card on a table). Cosy, lifestyle feel.

**Option C — The "We Make This" Behind the Scenes**
Quick 15-second clip showing: hands designing → printer running → finished product. Tells the story in one go.

### What to Include

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **First 2 seconds** | Hook — something visual that stops the scroll (satisfying print reveal, bold product close-up, movement) |
| **Product shown** | Pick your best-seller or most visually striking item |
| **Branding** | Mint/peach colours where possible, Paca Prints name visible at some point |
| **Text overlay** | Keep it short — e.g. "Personalised prints & gifts, made with love 🌿" or similar |
| **No face needed** | Hands-only is totally fine |
| **Sound** | A gentle trending audio or royalty-free background track — Meta boosts content with audio |
| **End frame** | Logo or "Shop at pacaprints.com" — just a second or two |

### What to Avoid

- Stock footage or overly polished, corporate-looking content — it won't feel like us
- Cluttered or busy backgrounds
- Too much text — people won't read it
- Captions that say "click the link" (Meta restricts this)

---

## Static Image Brief (Backup)

If a video isn't ready in time, use a clean product flatlay:

- One hero product (e.g. a popular print, a card, a gift bundle)
- Mint or peach background or neutral/natural surface (wood, linen)
- Good natural light
- Minimal props — keep the focus on the product
- Logo in one corner, small
- One line of text max (e.g. "Personalised gifts & prints — made in Bournemouth")

---

## Copy (What Goes in the Ad Text)

**Primary text (above the image/video):**
> Prints and personalised gifts made with love — for every occasion, every person, and every budget. 🌿

**Headline (bold line under the image):**
> Paca Prints — Personalised Gifts & Prints

**Call to action button:** Shop Now → pacaprints.com

---

## What We're Watching

This is a test. We'll look at:
- **Reach** — how many unique people saw it
- **CPM** — cost per 1,000 impressions (good if under £12)
- Any bump in website visits or follows during the run period

We are **not** judging this by sales. It's awareness. If CPM is good and reach is solid, we try a bigger budget next time.

---

## Timeline

- **Ad runs for:** 7–10 days
- **Daily budget:** ~£5–7/day
- **Campaign objective:** Reach or Brand Awareness (not Traffic or Conversions)
