## Product Categories

### Cards (Humour / Occasion)
High volume, low AOV (~£3.50–£6), high margin. TikTok's sweet spot.
- Birthday, Mother's Day, Valentine's, occasion cards
- Best examples: Derek Vday, Thanks for not swallowing me, Tena mum card

### Prints (Digital / Humour)
Medium volume, medium AOV (~£10–15), near-100% margin.
- Wall art, humour prints, pop culture (F1, K-pop, Stranger Things)
- Best examples: F1 print, Dog description, Are you pooping

### Personalised Prints
Lower volume, higher AOV (£12–20), slightly lower margin (cost to produce).
- Family prints, portrait prints, name prints
- Best examples: Family print, Portrait family print, Mum photo print

### Bundles
Low volume, high AOV (£15–30), high margin. Seasonal.
- Teachers bundle (star performer), bridesmaid proposal, dinosaur nursery set

### Seasonal / Event
Concentrated sales windows — Valentine's, Mother's Day, teachers, Christmas, Easter.
- Plan product launches 4–6 weeks ahead of key dates

### Physical / Bought-in Items
Lowest margin (48–85%). Easter cup, candles, bow water bottle, tumbler.
- Adds AOV to orders but drags overall margin
- Worth keeping only if they drive basket add-ons

### Cricut / Kids
Chore charts, teeth brushing charts — small volume but zero marginal COGS if printed in-house.
